Toc Details

An engine oil pan is an automotive component with simpler functions. It is also referred to as an oil sump, oil tray, or oil reservoir. The oil pump has a pickup tube that hangs into the pan and sucks up oil. Once used, the engine oil falls back into the pan. Apart from storing the oil, the oil pan also plays an important role in cooling it. When a vehicle moves forward, air flows under the pan, which carries away the heat from the oil. Sometimes, to increase the surface area, cooling fins are fitted on the oil pan. Oil pans can be used for two and four-stroke IC engines. However, the engine part needs to operate properly. As the weight of modern engines are reducing and the need for fuel efficiency has increased, manufacturers are focusing on designing compact engine oil pan. This is most likely to fuel the demand for engine oil pan market for automotive during the forecast period.
